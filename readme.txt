How to setup
1. get a thunderboard EFR32BG22 
2. git clone 
3. flash silabs-cgm.hex
4. if the phone bonded the device previous, 
	please remove the device from the bluetooth setting  in the phone
5. open nRF Toolbox, click Continuous Glucose
6. connect silabs-CGM
7. click start session, the measurement is changing per 2 seconds
8. click stop session, the measurement will stop
9. click disconnect, the app will disconnect the device
